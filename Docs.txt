Input - 
	1. Principal Amount
	2. Duration in Months
	3. Interest Rate
	4. Table Format - 
		1. Monthly 
		2. Yearly

Output - 
	Table - 
		col 1 - Date or Duration
		col 2 - Principle Amount
		col 3 - Interest Amount
		col 4 - Total Amount = col 2 + col 3

Functions -
	1. Display - 
		i. Monthly Calculator
		ii. Yearly Calculator
	2. Calculation - 
		i. Monthly Calculator
		ii. Yearly Calculator
